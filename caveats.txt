CAVEATS IN BUILDING CIASDIS

The default section is 2 Mbyte.
If the forth doesn't accomodate this it terminates with error 7.

Depending on the needs you may need to grow lina by the -g option.

For the moment the definition of BAG not via DATA is problematic.
So BAG is redefined locally.

There is a defect in ciriscv : #-PREFIX is not a PREFIX.
For the moment it is properly defined in ciasdis.frt overriding
a faulty definition.
